Creating Waterfall Breakdown Table



1. On Power BI desktop under Modeling click on new table button
2. Type the Dax formula below in the Dax formula bar, click on the check mark, and save

Waterfall_Breakdown =
DATATABLE(
"Category", STRING,
"SortOrder", INTEGER,
{
--{"Change Pt Tx", 1},
{"Change Rec Rate", 2},
{"Change Tx Cost", 3},
{"Change Len Stay",4}, {"Change Rec Prob", 5}
}
